About Me

Chris Hayes

I have been a fan of computers since I was first given access to the family's Apple IIGS. It ignited a passion for tech, games, and creative outlets on machines. I built my first website in 1998 with HTLM for Dummies and a love of the Simpson's. That GeoCities page has faded into the lost history of the web (way back machine doesn't even have it).

I married my beautiful bride my senior year of college and we now have a 7 year old who is the best! We live in her hometown for the moment, but have no future plans to remain here forever. We love to travel to new places and experience new things.

I graduated with my BA in 2005, was in Church Ministry for 10 years, and then social work for 5 more. I know hard work and attention to detail are critical to success. I also am well versed in listening to a client and helping to come up with an appropriate solution.

The best way to reach me is through the form on the Contact Page. I am available for full-time or part-time web development (remote) and may be willing to relocate.
